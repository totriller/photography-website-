About Me

Thank you for visiting Totriller Photography! I have been one of the top local photographers in whiteville, NC for almost 5 years. My simple, clean, crisp, and colorful images uniquely capture moments. I use specialty prime lenses, skillful lighting, and careful editing for accurate color and proper exposure. I also mix natural light and off-camera studio strobes for soft and dynamic portrait lighting. Using modern techniques, you will always see the details of a bride’s white dress, as well as the beautiful colors of a sunset. My goal is always to capture genuine emotions using a relaxed, candid approach mixed with traditional posed portraits. I’ve had clients tell me I’m high-energy, fun, and great to work with. I’m always happy to try any ideas you like for the pictures. I do whatever I can to make sure my clients are happy and keep my excellent reputation
